live well >> connected

Whether you need a home that is pet friendly or an active community in a natural setting with walking trails, West Wind Place provides homes designed with the young professional in mind. Our apartments are loaded with high-end amenities like patios, energy-efficient LED lighting, quartz countertops and FREE WI-FI.

We are nestled in the quiet, beautiful suburbs of Allendale. West Wind Place apartments are conveniently located for commuters! Your apartment would be a short drive of both the city of Grand Rapids and the Lake Michigan beaches in Grand Haven and Holland.

Are you shopping for a home in the area or here for business? We offer short-term and corporate leases. Furnished apartments are available. Call our office today at 616-895-6000 to discuss your apartment options.
