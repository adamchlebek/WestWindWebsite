Floor Plans

West Wind Place offers five different apartment floor plans. Select a one bedroom or two bedroom layout, and then personalize it to make it all your own.

Apartments with Tech & Entertainment in Mind

Both one bedroom and two bedroom floor plans are designed for an open contemporary lifestyle with technology and entertainment in mind in all rooms and areas of the apartment. Patio or balcony storage available in all two bedroom units give you the space you need to move around and live well in your quiet, Allendale neighborhood.
